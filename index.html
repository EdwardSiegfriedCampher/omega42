About the Omega 42 Project

The Omega 42 Project is a metaphysical theory of everything that proposes a new way of understanding existence through geometric emergence. By framing the universe as a recursive, self-generating system, the project explores the harmony between science, philosophy, and consciousness. At its heart lies the **Omega 42 Construct**, a framework for addressing the largest questions of existence.

The project is an open-source collaboration, spearheaded by Edward S. Campher and several AI collaborators, including ChatGPT, DeepSeek, and Claude. This is a continuous journey of discovery, and the community is invited to contribute.
